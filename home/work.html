Babble Royale

2021
PC/MAC (Steam)
Everybody House Games

Babble Royale is an intense multiplayer free-for-all that combines word-making skill with fast-paced tactical and strategic action. Earn money and collect loot to upgrade your abilities, outrun the deadly hot zone, and outwit or outlast all your opponents to have the Last Word!

Babble Royale was made in collaboration with my son James, who is a brilliant game designer in his own right. We made Babble Royale for competitive gamers who like smart games, and casual gamers who like intense competition. The combination of classic word-making gameplay with fast-paced tactical action means the game is easy to learn but has an insanely high skill ceiling.

Babble is almost as much fun to watch as it is to play, and the game has attracted a lot of streamers on Twitch and YouTube. It has introduced a lot of videogame fans to the fun of word games and vice versa. We’ve been especially thrilled to watch Babble evolve a community of dedicated players who are exploring the game at an extremely high level.
