re-Evolution

2021
PC/MAC
Baby Robot Games

re-Evolution is a game jam videogame developed for the King Game Jam 2018.

The game is based under a roguelike gameplay were you will face two types of enemies, the minions which can damage you and a final boss, which has some special range attacks and a lethal way to kill you. The dungeons are randomly generated.

Unfortunately we weren't the winners from that edition.
